This document tracks the completion of the AI Protector Workshop, a security-first journey that trains builders, security engineers, and DevSecOps professionals to defend AI-powered applications. The program covers MCP servers, digital twins, serverless platforms, and Vercel edge services with hands-on labs and professional reporting.
